Make sure you read the following information before you continue:

Your consent is needed - to add your accounts from your chosen bank or banking provider you need to give consent. We'll have access for the next 90 days to the following data:
  • Account Name
  • Account Balance
  • Sort Code and Account Number
  • Account Transactions
Renew your permission every 90 days - In 90 day intervals, we'll ask you to log in to your chosen bank or provider so you can renew the permission you gave and continue to see that account within this banking app. Sometimes the interval we contact you at may differ depending on the banking provider.

See up to 1 year(s) of transactions and banking data - We'll be requesting your account, balance and transactions from your chosen banking provider as far back as 1 year(s). Remember, you can stop access at any time

How we'll use your data - We'll use your data as set out in the customer agreement you signed when you opened an account. This may include us assessing overall affordability from the information we have from your banking provider and sending personalised communications based on this data. But if you wish to opt out of future marketing activity then you can do this from within the banking app. Also, where required or authorised by law, we disclose your information to fraud prevention agencies/law enforcement.

Your data is of course safe with us - We' handle all of your banking data the same as we handle your existing data.

Confirm your sharing data with us - To continue with this process, you'll leave this app and confirm you're happy to share your data with us at your chosen bank or banking provider.
